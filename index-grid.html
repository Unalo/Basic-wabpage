Munich

is the capital and the most populated city in the German state of Bavaria, on the banks of the River Isar north of the Bavarian Alps. Munich is also the third largest city in Germany, after Berlin and Hamburg, and the 12th largest city in the European Union, with a population of around 1.5 million. The Munich Metropolitan Region is home to 6 million people.

The city is a major centre of art, technology, finance, publishing, culture, innovation, education, business, and tourism in Germany and Europe and enjoys a very high standard and quality of living, reaching first in Germany and fourth worldwide according to the 2015 Mercer survey. According to the Globalization and World Rankings Research Institute Munich is considered an alpha-world city, as of 2015

Parliament

Parliament comsists of two Houses called the National assembly and National council of Provinces, Each house has its own disticts and function. The constitution instruct Parliament to facilitate public participation in law making process and to take reasonable measures for public access to its committee meeting and house. The bill of Right guarantees the right to assemble, demonstrate, picket and present petition as long as this is done in a peaceful way and the protestes are unarmed.

castle of Good Hope

Its a bastion fort built in the 17th century in Cape Town, South Africa. Originally located on the coastline of table bay, Following land reclaration the fort is now located inland. In 1936 the castle was declared a historic monument (now a provincial heritage site) and following restoration in the 1980s, it is considered the best preserved example of Dutch east India company fort.
